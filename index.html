Overview

The exterior of the Laptop is build completely with a high grade plastic which feels Premium in hand.Where as the interior of the laptop is built using Aluminium Alloy which makes the laptop flexes and keyboard flexes equal to Null.Due the Plastic build on the exterior and metal build in the interior the laptop weighs around 2.3Kgs.

Technical Specifications

The Asus Tuf F15 is powered by Intel Core i5-10300H Processor with 4 Cores and 8 Threads with maximun boost upto 4.4Ghz of the clock speed.The biggest plus point of the laptop is the presence of Dedicated Graphics Card i.e Nvidia GTX 1650Ti with 4GB of memory.This is one of the best midrange chip availaible in the market,which easily performs all the basic day-day activities such as Coding,Watching Videos,Internet Surfing,and gaming some AAA titles on medium settings(More on GAMING in the next section).

The Laptop ships with 8GB of DDR4 RAM with 2 slots out of which 1 slot is pre-occupied which can be extended upto 32GB.And it ships with a 512GB SSD With an additional M.2 slot for further SSD Extension upto 2TB and an Additional HDD slot which can extend upto 6TB

The keyboard of the laptop is a soft Chiklet Keyboard with RBG lighting. The RGB backlit resembles the Gaming experience on this Laptop and is Fully customizable using ASUS's in-built software ARMOURY CRATE.The screen size of the laptop is 15.6 inches LCD Display with a brightness of 250 nits with resolution of 1080p and an amazing Refresh Rate of 144Hz.

Gaming and Battery Performance

As the laptop has a 4GB dedicated Grphics memory gaming on this laptop with a 144Hz screen is just Amazing.I tested out Games such as Forza Horizon 4,GTA V,Red Dead Detemption 2,COD Warzone. and while playing these games there wasn't a single bit of lag or a single frame dropped,and the average FPS for these games were between 80-90(Games were played while charging) which is great for laptop below 60k

The laptop has an 78 WHr Battery,which is decent enough to get through 4-5 hours on light usage such as watching videos and internet surfing. And on heavy use the battery struggles and the backup drops down to 1.5-2Hrs of Screen on Time.
